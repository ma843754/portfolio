Case Study

Trip Planner App

A travel planning interface designed to help users organize activities, manage schedules, and keep important trip details in one clear, easy-to-use place.

Role

Project Manager, UX/UI Designer

Tools

React (Vite), Firebase, Weather API, LocalStorage

Focus

Organization, usability, task flow

  • Overview
  • Problem
  • Users
  • Goals
  • Final UI
  • Outcome
  • Reflection

Overview

The Trip Planner app was created to simplify the travel planning process by bringing activities, schedules, budgets, and trip details into one interface. The goal was to reduce the need to switch between multiple apps, notes, and tabs while planning a trip.

Problem

Planning a trip often means juggling scattered information across notes, booking confirmations, calendars, and travel apps. This can make it difficult to stay organized, keep track of priorities, and clearly visualize the full itinerary.

Users

  • Students and young travelers
  • People planning group trips
  • Users who want simple itinerary management

Design Goals

  • Simplify adding, organizing, and viewing trip activities
  • Create a clear visual timeline for schedules and plans
  • Reduce clutter by centralizing trip information in one place
  • Make the interface intuitive and easy to scan quickly

Final UI

The final interface emphasizes clarity through spacing, hierarchy, and consistent layout patterns. Key actions are easy to find, and the structure was designed to support fast planning, simple updates, and a more organized view of trip details.

Outcome

The design improves organization and reduces cognitive load by centralizing trip information into a single, easy-to-use interface. The result is a planning experience that feels clearer, more manageable, and more visually structured.

Reflection

If I continued developing this project, I would focus on user testing, collaboration features, and further refinement of the timeline experience. I would also explore ways to make budgeting and itinerary updates feel even more seamless.
