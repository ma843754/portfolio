About Me

I’m a UX/UI designer focused on creating intuitive, engaging digital experiences with strong visual clarity and thoughtful interaction design. I’m currently a UCF Digital Media student with a focus on UX/UI design, where I’ve developed a strong interest in creating user-centered interfaces that balance usability, accessibility, and visual appeal.

My work is driven by curiosity and problem-solving. I enjoy taking complex ideas and turning them into clear, usable solutions through research, wireframing, and iterative design. Whether I’m building a full interface or refining small interaction details, I aim to create experiences that feel natural, purposeful, and visually cohesive.

I have experience working with tools like Figma, HTML, CSS, and React, which allows me to bridge the gap between design and development. I value collaboration, feedback, and continuous improvement, and I’m always looking for ways to grow through testing, iteration, and thoughtful design decisions.

Hello, I’m Marcella Roberts.

I enjoy solving design problems through clean layouts, user-centered thinking, and intentional visual systems. My work is centered on creating interfaces that feel polished, easy to use, and meaningful for the people interacting with them.

I’m especially interested in designing experiences that balance function, clarity, and personality. As I continue growing as a designer, I hope to create thoughtful digital products that make everyday experiences simpler, more engaging, and more accessible.
